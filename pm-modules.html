MODULE 07  FAQ  ·  After testimonials, before lead form
FAQ

Common landlord questions.

Answered plainly. If your question isn't here, call Gwilym directly.

📞 02 9413 1183
How much does property management cost?
AGC's management fee is competitive with the inner-Sydney market. We charge a flat percentage of rent collected — no hidden fees, no "statement fees", no inspection add-ons. We'll give you a fee quote in writing within 24 hours of enquiry.
How do I switch from my current property manager?
The process takes 30–60 days depending on your existing agreement. AGC handles the full transition — notice to your current agent, document handover, and tenant communication. You don't manage the switch; we do.
Who actually manages my property day-to-day?
Gwilym Chan manages the residential portfolio. Alan Chan handles commercial properties and administration. Your property is never handed to a junior PM or managed by someone you haven't met. The directors are your point of contact.
How quickly do you find tenants?
AGC's average is 9 days to place a quality tenant across the current portfolio. We advertise on Domain and realestate.com.au, plus our existing network of qualified applicants from previous vacancies in the area.
Do you manage properties outside Waterloo?
Yes. Our core area covers Waterloo, Redfern, Surry Hills, Zetland, Alexandria, and Mascot. We also manage properties across broader inner-city Sydney. Call to confirm your suburb — if we can't manage it well, we'll say so.
Can you speak Cantonese or Mandarin?
Yes. Both Gwilym and Alan are fluent in Cantonese and Mandarin as well as English. If you prefer to discuss your property in either language, just let us know when you call.
